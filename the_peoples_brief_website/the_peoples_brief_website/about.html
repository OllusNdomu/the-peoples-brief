About Us

Learn more about our mission, vision and the team behind The People's Brief.

Our Mission

The People's Brief exists to amplify the voices of ordinary Zambians and provide unbiased reporting on the issues that matter most. Our mission is to deliver fact‑checked news, insightful analysis and thoughtful opinion pieces that empower our readers to stay informed and engaged.

Our Vision

We envision a Zambia where everyone has access to timely, accurate information and where journalism serves as a catalyst for positive change. Through digital storytelling, we aim to bridge communities, foster public dialogue and inspire action.

Our Team

The People's Brief is run by a diverse team of journalists, editors and contributors who are passionate about telling Zambia’s stories. From experienced reporters to fresh voices, our team is united by a commitment to ethical journalism and community engagement.

About Us

The People's Brief is an independent digital news platform amplifying the voices of ordinary Zambians. We deliver credible journalism, in‑depth reporting and thoughtful commentary that keeps you informed.
